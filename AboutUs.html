About Us

Our Story

We are students of University of Cebu-Main Campus. On our WEBDEV11, we were tasked to make an e-commerce site for our Semi-Final and Final Project altogether and as Information Technology students, there were no more second thoughts that we came up with an idea of making an e-commerce site religiously just for computers, it didn't take much decision-making into coming up with this idea for our website as it strongly correlates to our course, another reason for deciding to create an offline computer/laptop shopping site is that it doesn't need to have a lot of categories like Shopee for example where there is more than ten(10) category of products to be listed, we wanted to create an only one field e-commerce site, products strictly only for computers and laptops, third one and the last one is the aesthetics into making a computer ecommerce site. We subtly worked on our frontend designing into making the website look futuristic and "gamer-y", adding active rgb animation with a black background theme. All in all, we feel very accomplished for what we have created and especially the functions of the website which we have Alexa De Luna to thank for, she deserves all the credit.
